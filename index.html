Features

Open Source / Free

Completely free (GPL License) for personal use, customizable, and hosted on GitHub.

Manage Photos

Find, Search, and Organize all of your family photos

Archive Social Media

Our system will keep a copy of everything your family posts on a variety of different social media website.

  • Facebook
  • Twitter *
  • Dropbox *
  • Instagram *

Manage Music

Search, Organize, and play your music

Manage Movies

Search, Organize, and Watch your movies. Chromecast streaming coming soon

WebDav

Along with the web interface you can browse all of your content through a mapped WebDav drive.

Enterprise Foundation

This application is built on the same Open Source projects that power the Adobe Digital Asset Manager - Apache Jackrabbit, Felix (OSGI), and Sling project

Secure

Every piece of content can be secured and hidden from other family members.

Local

Keep your data local, everything is stored locally on your home computer or off a USB drive. Nothing in the cloud
